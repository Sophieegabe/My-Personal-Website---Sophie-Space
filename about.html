ABOUT ME

Hi there!


i'm Sophie from Georgia, currently living in Germany as a culinary school student, serial hobbyist, beginner content creator, writer, designer, and coder.

do you see that little girl dancing ridiculously to Selena Gomez's Tell Me Something I Don't Know? yep, that was me, performing in front of relatives and my little brother on my birthday! remembering how free, joyful, and dreamy she was, i wanted to bring that childish spirit back - the one that faded over time. so, this website is part of that plan!


this is a cute, pinky space where i share my ideas, writing, coding & design projects, resources, and all the random hobbies i pick up along the way.


i hope my little corner of the internet inspires you to create something that makes your inner child happy too!


and if you share the same interests as me (listed below), feel free to reach out - i'd love to connect!
